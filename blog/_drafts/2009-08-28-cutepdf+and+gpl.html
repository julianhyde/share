Once you've installed it, there is an extra printer called 'CutePDF Writer', and when you print to it, it writes to a PDF file.

CutePDF is freeware. Free, meaning that you can download it without paying a cent. However, it doesn't come with the freedoms of true open-source software, such as being able to view the source code, modify it, recompile, and redistribute the improved application.
